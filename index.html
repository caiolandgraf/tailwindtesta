Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the capital of France?

The capital of France is Paris. It's known for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

2. How many continents are there on Earth?

There are seven continents on Earth: Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Europe, North America, Australia (Oceania), and South America.

3. Who wrote "Romeo and Juliet"?

William Shakespeare wrote "Romeo and Juliet". It's one of his most famous plays and was written in the late 16th century.
